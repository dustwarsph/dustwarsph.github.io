What is Dust Wars?

Dust Wars is a fast-paced card game for 2–4 players. Players summon units, play event cards, and contest objectives. Matches take around 20–45 minutes and emphasize tactical positioning and timing.

  • 2–4 players
  • 20–45 minute playtime
  • Easy to learn, deep to master

Who is the creator?

Nate is an independent game designer and illustrator who enjoys crafting small but deep card games. Nate focuses on approachable mechanics, strong visual identity, and tabletop moments that create stories among players.

How it all started?

Dust Wars Ph began as a backyard tabletop experiment. What started as a few hand-drawn cards grew into a balanced deck-building experience tested by friends. The core idea was to create quick tactical rounds with memorable characters and art that sparks the imagination.

How to play?

  1. Each player draws a starting hand of 5 cards.
  2. On your turn, gain action points, play cards, and resolve effects.
  3. Use unit positioning and events to outmaneuver opponents.
  4. First player to reach the victory condition wins.

This is a short summary. Full rules come with the box and downloadable PDF.
